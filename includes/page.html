ABOUT ME

As a kid I enjoyed learning about new technologies. I used to take everyhting apart, which led to me deconstructing my first computer. I mostly taught myself about how computers worked, which brought me to where I am today. I usually do some sort of new project every month to keep me learning about new technologies. I have been interested in programming since I was a kid. Outside of school I am teaching myself PHP, Python, C++. C, Ruby, and various other useful things.

GITA is a four year program offered at Brea Olinda High School. This course teaches students four popular computer programming languages as well as other useful skills for being successful. We often have speakers from local businesses, or visit businesses. This allows us to learn more about what we can do to become more successful.
